Genotype

This game is simply called "botany".
The idea of this game is to work with your randomly generated plants
and breed them selectively until you have created the perfect, prosperous plant.
To do this, click two plants to breed, and hit the "next generation" button

Congratulations, you were able to get a plant
that has an average >= 13 on each of its' characteristics!
This plant will form a great foundation for the farms here
and shall provide food for you and illuminate much of
the genealogy of this place.
