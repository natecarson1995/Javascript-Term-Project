This game is called chemistry.
The idea of this game is to work with your randomly generated elements and compounds
and chemically separate, combine, and fuse them in order to create as many new chemicals as possible
To do this, drag compounds or elements from the bottom panel, into one of the input slots.
To switch tools, you simply click the specific tool on the top panel.

Congratulations, you were amass at least 30 new compounds
and elements!
This will surely help along the field of chemistry on this planet
and has shed light on some important things that may be useful in other fields.
Good work!
